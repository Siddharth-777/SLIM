How It Works

Our DO risk estimation model uses scientific correlations between water temperature, turbidity, and dissolved oxygen levels. No expensive DO sensors required!

Key Factors:
  • Higher temperature = Lower oxygen solubility
  • High turbidity = Reduced photosynthesis
  • Rapid changes = Potential oxygen stress

Risk Categories

MEDIUM RISK: Oxygen levels between 4-6 mg/L. Monitor closely for stress signs.
